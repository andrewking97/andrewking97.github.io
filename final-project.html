Final Project

For our CSC 106 final project, my partner and I made a sidescroller game called RocketMan. Click the start button to enter the game, and then press any key to start flying. When you are holding a key, RocketMan will fly upward, and if you release the key he will start to fall. If you hit the top of the screen, the ground, or any of the pipes, you lose! Collect green gems for bonus points!
